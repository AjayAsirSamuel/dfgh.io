About DUKE

DUKE is at the forefront of AI-powered safety vehicles, developing cutting-edge autonomous driving solutions that ensure a safer, smarter driving experience. With a mission to reduce traffic accidents and save lives, DUKE integrates AI, machine learning, and sensor technologies to create vehicles that are not only intelligent but also highly reliable and safe.
